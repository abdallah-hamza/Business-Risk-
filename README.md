📊 Business & Risk Dashboard - Power BI Project

📌 Overview
This project presents a **Business & Risk Analysis Dashboard** built using Power BI to monitor sales performance, cash flow, and loan risk.

It helps stakeholders understand:
- Revenue performance
- Cash inflow behavior
- Loan exposure and risk
- Payment method impact on business


🎯 Objectives
- Track total sales and revenue distribution
- Monitor cash inflows across different platforms
- Analyze loan vs cash transactions
- Identify overdue and remaining balances
- Support business decisions with data-driven insights


 📊 Dashboard Pages

🔹 1. Overview Dashboard
Provides a high-level summary of business performance:
- 💰 Total Sales: **755M**
- 💵 Total Collected: **581M**
- ⚠️ Total Remaining: **174M**
- 📦 Total Orders: **18K**
- 🏦 Total Loans: **10K**

Insights:
- A significant portion of revenue is still **uncollected (risk area)**
- Installments dominate payment methods compared to cash
- Sales distribution varies across branches and product models


🔹 2. Cash Dashboard
Focuses on cash flow analysis:
- 💰 Total Cash Inflow: **393.8M**
- 🧾 Cash Orders: **7K**

Key Analysis:
- Sales platforms comparison (Mobile App, In-Store, Website)
- Branch-level cash performance
- Quarterly cash inflow trends
- Product-level contribution to cash flow

Insights:
- Mobile App generates the highest cash inflow
- Website contributes the least → potential growth opportunity
- Stable cash trend across quarters

🔹 3. Loans Dashboard
Focuses on loan portfolio and risk analysis:
💰 Total Remaining Balance: 174M
📊 Total Loans Count: 10K
⚠️ Rate Overdue +90 Days: 4.68%

Key Analysis:
Quarterly trend of total remaining balance and number of loans
City-wise comparison of remaining balance and overdue rates
Risk segmentation (Low, Medium, High)
Monthly relationship between loan term and remaining balance

Insights:
Majority of loans fall under Low Risk category (~85%)
Giza shows the highest overdue rate among cities
Loan volume and remaining balance are stable across quarters
Slight correlation between longer loan terms and higher remaining balances

📈 Key Features
- KPI cards for quick insights
- Sales breakdown by:
  - Branch
  - Product Model
  - Payment Method
- Time-based analysis (Quarterly trends)


🛠 Tools & Technologies
- Power BI
- DAX (Data Analysis Expressions)
- Data Modeling
- Power Query


🧠 Key Insights
- High dependency on installment payments increases financial risk
- Outstanding balances (174M) indicate potential collection issues
- Mobile platform is the strongest revenue driver
- Some branches underperform compared to others
